Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Presto ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Photos you choose to restore. The App processes photos that you select from your device. These photos are sent to our cloud service for restoration. They are not stored permanently on our servers and are deleted shortly after processing is complete. The restored result is returned to your device and may be temporarily cached locally for sharing purposes.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, device ID, location, or any other personal information.

We do not collect, store, or share your original or restored photos beyond the immediate processing needed to provide the service.

Data Security

All image uploads and downloads between the App and our cloud service are transmitted over HTTPS encryption. Restored images cached on your device are stored in the app's private directory and are not accessible by other apps.

In-App Purchases

The App offers credits for purchase through Google Play's billing system. All purchase transactions are handled by Google Play and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Data Storage

Your credit balance is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. Restored images are temporarily cached on your device for sharing and can be cleared by the user at any time. We do not maintain accounts or cloud storage of your data.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, which operate under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services (in-app billing)
  • Cloudflare Workers (image processing)

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
